Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FamilyConnect

How does profile matching work?

When a new family member signs up and enters their name and parents, our smart system checks for existing profiles. If we find a match, we'll ask them to confirm if it's them. This prevents duplicate profiles and keeps your family tree organized.

Can I control who sees my information?

Absolutely! You have full control over your privacy settings. Choose what information is visible to family members, and control who can contact you. Your data is always secure and private.

How many family members can I add?

It depends on your plan. The Free plan allows up to 50 members, Family plan up to 500, and Unlimited plan has no restrictions. You can upgrade anytime as your family tree grows.

Can I print my family tree?

Yes! With paid plans, you can create beautiful printable family trees. Choose from multiple templates, customize what to include, select your starting point (patriarch, matriarch, or any family member), and download high-quality PDFs.

Is my family's data secure?

Security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption, regular security audits, and follow industry best practices. Your family data is private and will never be shared with third parties.

What if I need help getting started?

We offer comprehensive guides, video tutorials, and email support for all users. Unlimited plan subscribers get priority support with faster response times.
